About

I am a Postdocoral Alexander von Humboldt Fellow at the Fritz-Haber Institut in Berlin, Germany. I previously completed my PhD at the University of Cambridge with h Prof. Andrew Morris. My current work focuses on using machine learning techniques to predict spectroscopic signatures of Li-ion battery materials. I hope to combine experimental spectroscopy, first principles calculations, and machine learning to accelerate the speed with which we can understand new energy materials.
